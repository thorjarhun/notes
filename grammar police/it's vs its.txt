Term: It's
Def: A contraction for it is or it has.
Mnemonic: It's an apostrophe.

Term: Its
Def: The possessive form of the neuter pronoun "it".
Mnemonic: The possessive dog had its tail removed.
Rule of Thumb: If you can replace "it" with "his" or "her", there's no apostrophe.

Phrase that demonstrates both rules: It's not its.
